Notice to Vacate

Submit your notice when you're planning to move out from your rental property.

Notice to Vacate Information

Required Notice Period

According to New Zealand tenancy law and your rental agreement, you must provide:

  • Fixed-term tenancies: Notice to vacate at the end of a fixed term should be given at least 21 days before the end date.
  • Periodic tenancies: At least 21 days' written notice is required.

Failing to provide adequate notice may result in additional rent charges.

Move-Out Checklist

To ensure a smooth move-out process and maximize your bond refund, please complete the following checklist before vacating:

Cleaning

  • Thoroughly clean all rooms, including floors, walls, and windows
  • Clean kitchen appliances inside and out
  • Clean and sanitize bathroom fixtures
  • Remove all personal items from drawers and storage areas
  • Vacuum and clean carpets

Utilities & Services

  • Transfer or disconnect utilities (electricity, gas, water)
  • Provide forwarding address for mail
  • Cancel or transfer internet service
  • Return all keys, access cards, and remotes

Final Inspection

  • Schedule final inspection with property manager
  • Take photos of clean, vacated property
  • Complete property condition report
  • Provide bank details for bond refund

For a complete move-out guide, please review your lease agreement or contact your property manager.
