Submit Your Notice Request

Submission Tips

  • Submit lease extension requests at least 30 days before your current lease expires.
  • Provide as much detail as possible about your request to help us process it efficiently.
  • If you're requesting to add a roommate, they'll need to complete an application and screening.
  • For move-out notices, please use our dedicated "Notice to Vacate" form instead.

Notice Request Submitted

Thank you for submitting your notice request. We've received your information and will process it promptly.

What happens next?

  • You'll receive a confirmation email with your request details.
  • Your property manager will review your request within 2-3 business days.
  • We'll contact you to discuss your request and any additional steps required.

If you have questions about your request, please contact your property manager or our office at (09) 123-4567.

What to Expect After Submission

1

Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirmation within 24 hours acknowledging receipt of your request.

2

Review

Your property manager will review your request and contact you within 2-3 business days to discuss details.

3

Resolution

If approved, we'll prepare any necessary documentation and coordinate next steps with you.
