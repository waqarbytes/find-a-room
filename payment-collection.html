Payment Collection Features

Our comprehensive payment solution helps landlords collect rent on time and manage finances efficiently.

Automated Rent Collection

Set up automatic rent collection from tenants through bank transfers, credit cards, or direct debits, ensuring consistent cash flow without manual intervention.

Payment Reminders

Automated payment reminders sent to tenants via email or SMS before rent is due, reducing late payments and follow-up communications.

Financial Reporting

Comprehensive financial reports showing income, expenses, and cash flow for all your properties, exportable for accounting purposes.

Digital Receipts

Automatic generation of digital receipts for all payments, providing documentation for both landlords and tenants.

Secure Processing

Bank-level security for all financial transactions, ensuring the safety of your rental income and tenant payment information.

Late Fee Management

Configurable late fee settings with automatic calculation and application according to your lease terms.

How Our Payment System Works

A simple, efficient process for collecting and managing rental payments

Setup Tenants

Add your tenants and properties to the system. Specify rent amounts, due dates, and payment methods for each tenant.

Schedule Payments

Set up recurring payment schedules. Tenants receive automatic notifications when payments are due or overdue.

Receive Funds

Payments are automatically processed and deposited into your designated bank account, typically within 1-2 business days.

Track Payments

Monitor all payments through your dashboard. View payment history, outstanding balances, and transaction details.

Generate Reports

Create detailed financial reports for accounting, tax purposes, or general financial planning and analysis.

Manage Expenses

Track and categorize property expenses, maintaining a complete financial picture of your rental business.

Tenant Payment Portal

Tenants access a dedicated portal where they can:

  • View current and past rent payments
  • Make one-time payments for rent or other fees
  • Set up automatic payments for recurring rent
  • Update payment methods and personal information
  • Download payment receipts and rent statements
  • Communication with property management

The portal is mobile-friendly, allowing tenants to manage their payments from any device, anytime, anywhere.

Flexible Payment Options

We offer multiple payment methods to accommodate tenant preferences:

  • Bank Transfers (ACH)

    Connect bank accounts for direct, fee-free transfers

  • Credit/Debit Cards

    Secure credit and debit card processing (convenience fee may apply)

  • Recurring Payments

    Set-and-forget automatic payments on the rent due date

  • Cash Payment Network

    Options for cash payments at participating retail locations

Smart Notification System

Our communication system keeps tenants informed about their payments:

  • Payment Reminders

    Customizable reminders sent 5, 3, and 1 day before rent is due

  • Payment Confirmations

    Immediate confirmation when payments are received

  • Late Payment Alerts

    Automatic notifications when payments are overdue

  • Notification Preferences

    Tenants can choose email, SMS, or both for notifications
