Streamlined Rental Applications

At Find a Room, we prioritize a smooth and efficient rental experience for both tenants and landlords. To achieve this, we leverage the secure and user-friendly Tenant Portal on tenant.co.nz for all rental applications.

Why Tenant Portal?

Convenience

The online portal allows you to submit your application electronically from anywhere, at any time. No need for printing or mailing paperwork.

Security

Tenant Portal ensures your personal information is protected with industry-leading security protocols.

Streamlined Process

The portal guides you through each step, simplifying the application process and ensuring you provide all necessary information.

Faster Processing

Landlords receive your application electronically, allowing for quicker review and communication.

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your rental application

1

Create an Account

Register for a Tenant.co.nz account by providing your email address and creating a password. This secure account will allow you to save and track your applications.

2

Complete Personal Information

Enter your personal details, including contact information, identification, employment history, and references. This information will be securely stored for future applications.

3

Upload Supporting Documents

Upload required documentation such as ID, proof of income, and references. Digital copies of these documents can be easily attached to your application.

4

Select Property and Submit

Choose the specific property you're applying for from our listings and submit your completed application. You'll receive an email confirmation of your submission.

5

Application Review

The property manager will review your application and conduct necessary checks. This process typically takes 1-3 business days depending on reference availability.

6

Receive Notification

You'll be notified of the application outcome via email. If approved, you'll receive instructions for the next steps, including lease signing and payment details.
