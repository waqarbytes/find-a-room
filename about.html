About Find A Room

Your trusted partner for quality shared living solutions across New Zealand.

Building Thriving Rental Communities

At Find a Room, we’re passionate about creating a better rental experience for everyone. We believe finding a place to live shouldn’t be stressful, and having roommates shouldn’t feel like a gamble. That’s why we built a platform that connects people with compatible roommates and streamlines the rental process for both tenants and landlords.

WHAT DRIVES US

We are dedicated to creating better shared living experiences through professional property management, careful tenant matching, and excellent customer service.

Matching the Right People

We go beyond just finding a room; we help you find your perfect fit. Our innovative matching tools connect you with roommates who share your interests and lifestyle, fostering harmonious living environments.

Building Communities

We envision a world where renting isn’t just about a place to stay, but a foundation for building friendships and connections. Whether you’re a new tenant in a new city or looking to share your space, Find a Room helps you find your tribe.

Empowering Landlords & Tenants:

Landlords can find responsible tenants and manage their properties with ease. Tenants can discover their dream rentals and enjoy a stress-free search process.
